üî• What is Forest Fire Weather Index (FWI)?

The Forest Fire Weather Index (FWI) is a numeric rating of fire intensity that combines the rate of fire spread with the amount of fuel being consumed. It's a crucial tool used by fire management agencies worldwide to assess fire danger and make informed decisions about fire prevention and suppression.

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to predict FWI values based on various environmental parameters, helping authorities and researchers better understand and prepare for potential fire risks.
